My Top 7 Hiking Trails

My favorite hobby for the last ten years has been hiking. What started off as short treks (< 3 miles long) into the woods with nothing more than my sunglasses, has grown into packing a bag full of supplies and spending all day in the woods. Here is a list of my top 7 hikes within a few hours of St. Louis, MO.

  1. Auxier Ridge, Courthouse Rock & Double Arch Loop

    Daniel Boone National Forest | Kentucky | 7.3 miles

  2. Natural Bridge & Rock Garden Loop

    Daniel Boone National Forest | Kentucky | 3.75 miles

  3. Starved Rock Visitor's Center, St. Louis Canyon, & Wildcat Canyon

    Starved Rock State Park | Illinois | 4.85 miles

  4. Whispering Pines Trail

    Hawn State Park | Missouri | 7.3 miles

  5. Little Grand Canyon

    Shawnee National Forest | Illinois | 3.9 miles

  6. Trails 1, 3, 4, 5, 9, & 10

    Turkey Run State Park | Indiana | N/A

  7. Cave Spring Loop & Devil's Well

    Ozark National Secenic Riverways | Missouri | 5.6 miles
